CRYPTONITE

Cybersecurity Excellence at Manipal Institute of Technology

About Us

Cryptonite is the official cybersecurity and ethical hacking student project at Manipal Institute of Technology (MAHE). We advance cybersecurity through research, education, and hands-on practice across software and hardware security.

Mission

To explore cutting-edge security methodologies, analyze cryptographic systems, and build practical expertise through projects, competitions, and research initiatives.

What We Do

  • Capture‑The‑Flag (CTF) competitions and training
  • Security research in cryptography and systems
  • Hardware and software security analysis
  • Workshops, mentorship, and knowledge sharing

Initiatives

We organize flagship events such as Cryptober and niteCTF, bringing together the community for collaborative learning and innovation.
